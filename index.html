Buff MOMO
           For savory Chicken Momos, begin by mixing minced chicken with finely chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices in a large bowl. Let the mixture cool for 20-30 minutes. Meanwhile, prepare the dough by kneading plain flour, salt, and oil with warm water. After 3-4 hours of resting, divide the dough into small balls, roll out thin rounds, and fill them with the seasoned chicken mixture. Shape and steam the momos for 5-7 minutes. Serve these.
Chicken MOMO
           For flavorful Buff (beef) Momos, create a dough by combining plain flour, salt, and oil, kneading it with warm water. Allow the dough to rest for several hours, then divide it into small balls. Roll out thin rounds and use them to encase a well-seasoned minced buffalo meat mixture. Shape the momos and steam for 5-7 minutes until cooked through. Enjoy these hearty buffalo momos with a side of spicy dipping sauce for a satisfying culinary experience.
Veg MOMO
           For a delightful vegetarian option, prepare Veg Momos by combining finely chopped vegetables like cabbage, carrots, and bell peppers in a bowl. Add minced garlic, ginger, and a mix of spices to enhance the flavor. Let the vegetable mixture rest for 20-30 minutes. Knead a dough with plain flour, salt, and oil, and let it rest for 3-4 hours. Divide the dough into small balls, roll out thin rounds, and fill them with the vegetable mixture. Shape and serve.
